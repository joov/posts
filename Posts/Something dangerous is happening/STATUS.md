# Something dangerous is happening

## Status: Draft

## Created: 2026-03-28

## Description
Transfer of Matt Shumer's post "Something big is happening" to the dark side -- exploring AI-driven threats, from adversarial attacks on AI agents and AI-enhanced social engineering to the governance gap when AI agents act as "employees" without established controls. Covers whether traditional ISMS and risk-based approaches still hold, and what new defensive measures are emerging.
